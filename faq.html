Frequently Asked Questions

This exclusive partnership between Startup Garage and WeWork is about equipping

  • Does Startup Garage have connections to accelerators and investors outside of Hong Kong?
  • Through our APAC exclusive partnership with WeWork, we build connections to accelerators and investors throughout Asia.

  • Does Startup Garage focus on a particular type of business?
  • We are primarily focus on web­based business but do work across a wide range of industries. We can also build a full spectrum of web­based, iOS and Android apps to bring your concept to life.

  • If my startup does not have a tech component to it, can I apply anyways?
  • At the current time, we are more focused on web­based business. At the minimum, we can work with you to build a website & landing page for your potential customers. However, we may be open to helping businesses without a strong tech component later on.

  • Is there a cost to apply to Startup Garage’s preaccelerator program?

  • You will be invoiced upon your acceptance into our Startup Garage preaccelerator program. The price is HKD$80,000.

    At a reasonable fee, we provide:

    • A fast prototype that allows you to test the market and engage investors/accelerators ­
    • 3­month technical team for unlimited iteration on your prototype
    • 3­month WeWork work space ­
    • Guaranteed private meetings with a network of early­stage VCs and angels that will allow you to sustain your startup past the Startup Garage programme ­
    • A curated industry expert network that provides guidance and help tackle difficult decisions ­
    • Access to a private digital platform that directly connects you with 50,000+ members of the global WeWork community, in which you can: 1) promote your services and find new clients, 2) search for business and service listings that can help your startup grow, 3) network with like­minded entrepreneurs, 4) expose your startup to a sizable global audience from day one. ­ A community team that provides launch support such as: build initial user community, organize launch events, and connect with media

    ­ ­ You can apply here​.

  • I don’t have more than a "paper napkin" idea. Is Startup Garage’s program suitable for me?

  • Yes! Startup Garage is here to help you get those ideas to market with our 3 month programme. We are specifically designed for non­technical founders that are serious about transforming ideas into investment­ready ventures.

    However, we require you to have an “all­in” mindset – we work with our founders to become investment and/or accelerator ready in a very short amount of time by providing them prototype, technical team, investor network, and fundraising opportunity. For further details, please refer to How It Works.


  • Will Startup Garage provide seed funding for me?

  • Startup Garage does not provide seed funding or invest in your company. However, you will have the opportunity to raise capital at the end of the programme through the investor mini roadshows you can choose to launch and by talking to our angel investor network.

    We also help you tap into WeWork’s accelerators and investors connections throughout Asia.


  • Will Startup Garage invest in my company?
  • As mentioned above, Startup Garage does not provide seed funding or invest in your company but equips founders with the opportunity to raise their own capital through our channels and network.

  • Can I apply to Startup Garage’s program if I am not based in Hong Kong but in Asia?
  • Since most of the acceleration during this time will take place in Hong Kong, it is ideal for Founders to be based in Hong Kong to take full advantage of the services and resources available to them.

  • What happens after the completion of the Startup Garage programme?

  • At the completion of the programme, founders will have transformed their idea into a market­tested prototype and proven a product­market fit. This should sufficiently equip you to pitch to investors and accelerators. We hope you will take full flight through two potential channels we have for our founders who graduate:

    • Access to capital via angel investors or seed funding
    • Increased chance of acceptance to an Incubator / Accelerator

  • Is there a deadline for submitting an online application?
  • No, we accept applications on a rolling basis. Apply here.

  • What is the criteria for being accepted to Startup Garage’s programme?

  • Our main criteria for selecting applicants is how committed you are executing upon your startup idea and how to find the market­product fit within 90 days. We want to work with people who have an “all­in” mindset to make their business a success.

    If you do receive disappointing news regarding your application, do not fret! Our team will provide you some feedback on what aspects of your application we were concerned about and provide suggestions on ways you can improve. We are happy to review your application again if those issues can be addressed.


  • Why Startup Garage out of all the accelerators available in Hong Kong?

  • Startup Garage is a pre­accelerator, which is a new kind of startup programme specifically designed for non­technical founders. We aim to target the segment prior to being in a full fledged accelerator programme, and provide you a prototype, co­working space, investor network, and fundraising opportunity for you to find the product­market fit for your idea within 90 days.

    We are different from the accelerators available in Hong Kong as we do not take a stake in your business, but rather allow you the opportunity to fundraise through our existing channels of angel investors and connections to accelerators. We also do not have cohorts, but accept applicants on a rolling basis.


We Know Startup!

Startup’s product-to-market approach emphasizes on quick development of a “minimum viable product,” designed with the smallest set of features that will appeal to a small group of target audience.

You will then continue to experiment by tweaking your offerings, see how the market responds, and change your offerings accordingly. The goal is to accelerate the pace of designing a product and developing a market.
